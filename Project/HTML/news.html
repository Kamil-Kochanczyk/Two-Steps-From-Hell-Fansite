Latest news

"Bloom"

New single by Thomas Bergersen

10.11.2023

Bloom, a new single by Thomas Bergersen, was released on November 10, 2023. It is a song about the feeling that you have when you are next to the person you love. It features the voice of Thomas Bergersen himself.

Listen on Spotify: Bloom

"Rock The Lightning"

New single by Nick Phoenix

10.11.2023

Rock the Lightning is a new rock single by Nick Phoenix. It was released on November 10, 2023. Nick Phoenix also confirmed that he plans to make a music video for this song in the future.

Listen on Spotify: Rock The Lightning

"Am I Real"

New single by Thomas Bergersen

13.10.2023

Am I Real, Thomas Bergersen's new single, was realeased on October 13, 2023. It was made in collaboration with Boom Jinx, a Norwegian music producer and DJ. Video for this song is accompanied by a fictional story.

Listen on YouTube: Am I Real
